Adoption & Travels

How your new friend makes their way home.

Ready-made critters ship within 2-3 business days. For custom "made-to-order" friends, please allow 1-2 weeks for stitching and stuffing before they begin their journey.

We ship worldwide! Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days domestically. International travels can take 2-4 weeks depending on the destination.

Due to the handmade nature of our critters, we generally do not accept returns. However, if your friend arrives injured (damaged during travel), please contact us within 48 hours so we can make it right!
